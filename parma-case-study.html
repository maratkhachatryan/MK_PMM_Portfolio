Parma Online Digital Transformation

Customer Research & Integrated Marketing Communications Strategy

Executive Summary

Challenge: Transform Parma's underperforming online platform into a competitive e-commerce channel through data-driven customer insights and integrated marketing communications.

Approach: Conducted comprehensive market research including consumer survey (81 respondents), SWOT analysis, buyer persona development, and DAGMAR framework implementation to measure advertising effectiveness.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Time-saving convenience
  • Well-designed, user-friendly website
  • Product availability
  • Variety of options

Weaknesses

  • Poor online customer service
  • No exclusive products
  • Lack of advertising/promotions
  • Bad SEO (176 backlinks vs 500k needed)
  • Weak social media (883 Instagram followers)

Opportunities

  • Increased online demand (COVID-19)
  • Higher profit margins online
  • Faster delivery services
  • Network expansion potential

Threats

  • Internet connectivity issues
  • Major competitors (SAS: 152k FB likes)
  • Negative online reviews
  • Required marketing investment

DAGMAR Framework Implementation

Define: Increase online platform awareness and usage among 18-35 year old segment
Advertising Goals: Move 22% of target audience from unawareness to active usage
Measurement: Track website traffic, conversion rates, and repeat purchase behavior
Timeline: 6-month campaign during COVID-19 lockdown and summer period
Channels: Social media, mobile marketing, TV ads, direct marketing

Strategic Recommendations

1. Fix Brand Consistency
Unify logos across all platforms (currently 3 different versions), create consistent brand messaging, develop unique value proposition
2. Enhance Digital Presence
Implement A/B testing campaigns, invest in Google AdWords, improve SEO from 176 to 500k+ backlinks, develop content marketing strategy
3. Improve Customer Experience
Hire professional support team, implement live chat, create mobile app, add order tracking system
4. Create Exclusivity
Leverage exclusive imported wines, develop private label products, implement loyalty program with referral discounts
5. Social Media Revamp
Run targeted Facebook/Instagram campaigns, create engaging content calendar, implement influencer partnerships
6. Data-Driven Optimization
Hire data analyst for CRM insights, implement conversion tracking, conduct regular customer satisfaction surveys
