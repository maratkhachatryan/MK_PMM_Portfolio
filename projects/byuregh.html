Strategic Entry Approach

Product Localization
Renamed product to "نقي" (Pure) in Arabic, adapted labeling for local requirements, considered taste preferences for Middle Eastern market
Distribution Strategy
Direct distribution through local agents (legal requirement), partnership with Carrefour chain, compliance with Kuwait's Article 260 on commercial agencies
Competitive Positioning
Price parity with competitors (130-200 AMD/bottle) while emphasizing natural spring source vs costly desalination ($1.04/m³)
Cultural Adaptation
Relationship-oriented business approach, Arabic-first communication, influencer marketing via Instagram/Snapchat, Halal certification compliance
