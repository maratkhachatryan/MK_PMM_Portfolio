Apaga Projects

Buyer Persona Research & Marketing Strategy

Executive Summary

Challenge: Understand and differentiate customer groups for Yell Extreme Park and Apaga Resort in Yenokavan, Armenia.

Approach: Conducted 146 surveys, 20 in-depth interviews, and 3 days of on-site observations. Compared findings with Breavis and Grant Thornton reports. Validated personas via Facebook A/B testing.
