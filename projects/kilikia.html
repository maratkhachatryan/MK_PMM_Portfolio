Approach: Conducted comprehensive brand audit including market analysis, brand inventory assessment, consumer survey (85+ respondents), in-depth interviews, and competitive benchmarking against local and international competitors.

Key Findings

  • • Only 3-4 variants have significant consumer awareness
  • • Naming strategy uses target indicators (Youth), occasions (Celebrative), and nostalgia (1952)
  • • 8 of 12 products use identical packaging, creating differentiation challenges
  • • Price range: 390-490 AMD (positioned as mass-market, not premium)

Strategic Recommendations

1. Redesign Digital Presence
Complete website overhaul with modern design, product information, and e-commerce capabilities. Benchmark: Dargett's engaging digital experience.
2. Brand Element Refresh
Create memorable logo with higher recognition potential, develop brand slogan and jingle for consistency across touchpoints.
3. Portfolio Optimization
Line cutting: Eliminate underperforming variants. Line extension: Introduce flavored beers (apricot ale) to compete with Dargett.
4. Packaging Differentiation
Unique bottle designs for each variant, modernized labels maintaining heritage elements, innovative caps for youth segment.
5. Sub-branding Strategy
Create sub-brands like "Գավաթ by Kilikia" for Armenian trend appeal or "Raise by Kilikia" for international/modern positioning.
6. Expand Draft Offerings
Introduce draft versions of specialty beers beyond classic Kilikia to increase variant awareness and trial.
